Safety

How to stay safe around hornets?

Wear proper clothing: Light colors and long sleeves.

Avoid smells: No perfume or scented products.

Keep food sealed: Prevent attracting hornets.

Stay away from nests.

Tips

  • Do not remove nests yourself
  • Avoid high hornet activity areas
  • Keep windows closed or screened
