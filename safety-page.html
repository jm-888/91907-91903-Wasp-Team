SAFETY

Learn how to stay safe around hornets and reduce the risk of dangerous encounters.

How to stay safe around hornets?

Preventive Measures
  • Wear light-colored long sleeves and pants
  • Avoid perfumes or scented products
  • Keep food and drinks sealed outdoors
  • Stay away from hornet nests
If You Encounter a Hornet
  • Stay calm and do not swat
  • Move slowly away
  • If swarmed, cover your head and neck
If You Are Stung
  • Use vinegar to neutralize venom
  • Apply a cold compress
  • Seek medical help if symptoms are severe

TIPS

  • Never attempt to remove a hornet nest yourself
  • Avoid areas with frequent hornet activity
  • Seal sugary food and drinks outdoors
  • Keep windows and doors screened
