REPORT A SIGHTING

Help us track hornets in your area. Submit sightings quickly and easily so we can protect native bees and biodiversity.

Yellow-legged hornets are larger than bees, with yellow legs and an orange-brown body. Learn key features to identify them quickly!
Commonly found in northern New Zealand during summer months. They build large paper nests in trees and roofs.
These hornets threaten native bees and biodiversity, and their stings can be dangerous to humans and pets.
No harm in reporting! If you are unsure, check out how to Identify Hornets! and help protect NZ’s ecosystems.
