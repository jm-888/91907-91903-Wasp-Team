Why

Hornets can pose serious risks due to their venom, aggressive behavior, and ability to attack in groups.

Why hornets are harmful?

Toxic Venom Effects

Causes pain, swelling, and possible organ damage.

Allergic Reactions

Can trigger life-threatening anaphylaxis.

Aggressive Behavior

Hornets can sting repeatedly and attack in groups.

High-Risk Situations

Near nests or sugary food areas.

⚠ WARNING

  • Disturbing nests causes attacks
  • Swatting increases aggression
  • Sweet food attracts hornets
