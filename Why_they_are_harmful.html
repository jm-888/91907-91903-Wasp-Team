Why hornets are harmful?

Toxic Venom Effects: Hornet venom can cause severe pain, swelling, and in extreme cases organ failure.

Life-Threatening Allergies: Some people may experience fatal allergic reactions.

Aggressive Behavior: Hornets can sting multiple times and attack in groups.

High-Risk Situations: Near nests or food areas.

⚠ WARNING

  • Disturbing nests causes attacks
  • Swatting increases aggression
  • Sweet food attracts hornets
